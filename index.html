At InaiksBeats, we are always keen to work with the best new talent and if you believe that you have something which makes you stand out from the crowd, we would be very interested to hear from you. We have a great deal of experience and expertise when it comes to music production and we are proud to say that we have already worked with a lot of prominent and up and coming artists around the world. At this present time, InaiksBeats are working with artists in the UK, the USA and Canada but we are always looking for new ways to work alongside fresh and emerging talent.

We are proud to offer a completely bespoke service that provides completely new and original pieces of music for our clients. We can provide a completely tailored service which means that we will get to know you and listen to what you are trying to get across with your music. We aim to bring our music sampling, production and mastering skills to the process, providing you with the best platform to create the music that you want to hear and make.

When it comes to musical genres, we are comfortable working in a variety of different styles but we are most noted for our work in the Rap, Hip Hop, RnB, Reggae and Soul genres. This provides a lot of scope for the musicians and performers that we work with and we are keen to add to the roster of artists that we work with.
